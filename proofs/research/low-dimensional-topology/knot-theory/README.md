# Research / Low-Dimensional Topology / Knot Theory

A-class research-level proofs (2015+ papers) centered on knots, links, and braids.

## What belongs here

- Novel invariants or extensions of classical invariants (Khovanov, HOMFLY, colored Jones, categorifications)
- Structural theorems from post-2015 papers (quasipositivity, concordance, slice-genus bounds)
- Results mixing knot theory with adjacent fields (knot Floer homology, gauge theory, quantum topology)
- Recent conjectures and their partial resolutions (Volume conjecture progress, slice–ribbon, etc.)

## What does NOT belong here

- Textbook computations of Jones / Alexander for named knots → `proofs/library/low-dimensional-topology/knot-invariants/`
- Standard braid relations → `proofs/library/low-dimensional-topology/braid-group/`
- Undergraduate-level Reidemeister-move style arguments → library

## Naming convention

`{author-year-short}-{keyphrase}/` e.g. `piccirillo-2020-conway-knot-not-slice/`.

Each folder contains: `problem.md`, `proof.md`, `report.md`, `notes.md`, `failed_attempts/`.
