# Research / Low-Dimensional Topology / Mapping Class Groups

A-class results on MCG(S_{g,n}), its subgroups, and its actions.

## What belongs here

- Cohomology and quasi-morphisms of MCG (Bestvina–Fujiwara style)
- Nielsen–Thurston classification refinements
- Subgroup structure theorems (Torelli, Johnson filtration)
- MCG actions on curve complex / Teichmüller space (Masur–Minsky, Rafi)
- Stable commutator length, BMS-type rigidity

## What does NOT belong here

- Standard presentations of MCG (Wajnryb, Gervais) → library/dehn-twist-relations/
- Classical lantern/chain/braid relations as ISOLATED lemmas → library/dehn-twist-relations/
- Standard computation of MCG(S_{0,4}), MCG(T^2) → library

## Naming convention

`{author-year}-{short-result}/` — e.g. `mangahas-2013-uniform-uniform-exponential-growth/`.
