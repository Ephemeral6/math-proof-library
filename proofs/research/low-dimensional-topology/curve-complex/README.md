# Research / Low-Dimensional Topology / Curve Complex

A-class results on C(S), its hyperbolicity, its boundary, and subsurface projections.

## What belongs here

- Hyperbolicity of C(S) and uniform bounds (Aougab, Bowditch, Clay–Rafi–Schleimer, Hensel–Przytycki–Webb)
- Subsurface projection distance bounds (Masur–Minsky)
- Gromov boundary identifications (Klarreich, Schleimer)
- Distance computations and algorithms
- Asymptotic dimension bounds (Bestvina–Bromberg–Fujiwara)

## What does NOT belong here

- Toy finite subcomplex checks, flag/link verification → library/simplicial-complexes/
- Basic simplicial complex definitions → library/simplicial-complexes/

## Naming convention

`{author-year}-{short-result}/` — e.g. `bowditch-2014-uniform-hyperbolicity-curve-complex/`.

## Note

Proofs here typically rely on INFINITE simplicial complexes; TSV-Simplicial can only verify finite local neighborhoods. Any proof step that appeals to global hyperbolicity or asymptotic behavior must be flagged as outside TSV scope.
