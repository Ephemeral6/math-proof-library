# Research / Low-Dimensional Topology / 3-Manifolds

A-class results on 3-manifolds: geometry, topology, and algebraic invariants.

## What belongs here

- Geometrization consequences and applications (post-Perelman)
- Heegaard Floer homology, monopole Floer, embedded contact (post-2010)
- Virtual Haken / virtual fibering (Agol, Wise; post-2012)
- Heegaard splittings and their distances
- Hyperbolic volume inequalities and rigidity (post-2015)
- Dehn surgery and exceptional fillings

## What does NOT belong here

- Classical hyperbolic-geometry formulas → library/hyperbolic-geometry/
- JSJ decomposition as a black-box theorem → library
- Standard knot complements → library/knot-invariants/

## Naming convention

`{author-year}-{short-result}/` — e.g. `agol-2013-virtual-haken/`.

## Note

Many 3-manifold proofs depend on heavy machinery (Ricci flow, normal surface theory, Heegaard Floer). TSV cannot currently verify these. Use `[REF:external:theorem-name]` or `[UNVERIFIABLE: requires-{machinery}]` and document clearly.
