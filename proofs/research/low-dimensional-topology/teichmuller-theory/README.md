# Research / Low-Dimensional Topology / Teichmüller Theory

A-class results on Teichmüller space, its geometry, and its dynamics.

## What belongs here

- Teichmüller geodesic flow dynamics (Eskin–Mirzakhani–Mohammadi, Forni)
- Rigidity of Teichmüller geodesics (Rafi's combinatorial model)
- Moduli space volumes (Mirzakhani recursion)
- Holomorphic rigidity and extremal length bounds
- Connection to quadratic differentials and flat structures

## What does NOT belong here

- Standard quasiconformal map theory → library/hyperbolic-geometry/
- Uniformization for specific small-complexity surfaces → library

## Naming convention

`{author-year}-{short-result}/`.

## Note

Many Teichmüller-theory proofs rely on extremal length / moduli arguments that are outside TSV's automated verification scope. Document `[UNVERIFIABLE: extremal-length]` for such steps and require clear picture-to-formal translation in Fixer.
