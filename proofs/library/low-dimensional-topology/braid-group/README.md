# Library / Low-Dimensional Topology / Braid Group

B- and C-class reusable lemmas on B_n (Artin braid group) and its representations.

## What belongs here

- Artin presentation relations and their verification
- Markov moves and closures (Alexander's theorem, Markov's theorem — as black boxes with references)
- Burau representation properties
- Word-problem algorithms (Garside normal form for small n)
- Braid→permutation surjection π: B_n → S_n
- Pure braid group P_n structure (generators, lower central series)
- Center of B_n (cyclic, generated by Δ^2)

## TSV integration

`tsv/tsv_group.py` implements `check_braid_relation(sigma_i, sigma_j)` and word-problem checks for B_n with n ≤ 10. Library entries should be TSV-verified whenever in scope.
